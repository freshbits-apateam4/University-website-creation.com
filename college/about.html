About Us

We are the world's largest University

The University of Tamilnadu, established in 1977, holds the title of being one of the largest universities in the world by enrollment. Located in Tamilnadu, India, it boasts a sprawling campus and offers a diverse range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines. With thousands of students enrolled in its numerous affiliated colleges and institutions, the University of Tamilnadu plays a significant role in shaping the educational landscape of India and beyond. Its expansive infrastructure, esteemed faculty, and rich academic heritage contribute to its reputation as one of the premier educational institutions globally.
